Returns And Exchanges

We accept returns and exchanges within 30 days of the purchase date. Items must be in their original condition, unused, and with all tags attached. Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item received is defective or incorrect.

Refunds

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method upon receipt and inspection of the returned item. Please allow up to 7 business days for the refund to be processed.

Damaged Or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately for assistance. We will arrange for a replacement or refund as soon as possible.

Shipping

We offer shipping within the United Kingdom and select international destinations. Shipping costs and delivery times may vary depending on the destination and selected shipping method.

Privacy

We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. Your information will only be used for order processing and communication purposes and will not be shared with third parties.
